This Employment Agreement is entered into between TechCorp Inc. and the Employee. Employee agrees to assign all intellectual property created during employment and for two years after termination to TechCorp Inc. Employee agrees not to work for any competitor within a 50 mile radius for two years post-employment. All disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration and Employee waives all rights to jury trial and class action. TechCorp reserves the right to modify compensation, role, and responsibilities at any time without notice or consent. Employee consents to monitoring of all devices, communications, and accounts including personal devices used for any work purpose. This agreement auto-renews annually unless terminated with 90 days written notice by Employee only. Employee agrees to pay liquidated damages of $25,000 if they resign within 12 months of signing.
